Christian Fernandez Farjat
CEO & Founder

Christian is a successful businessman who has founded several companies. Among them is CLinside, a start-up service company at the Atucha II Nuclear Power Plant. He also founded Hydroner Servicios Petroleros, specialized in water transfer for hydraulic fracturing.
